Support Vesperium

Vesperium began and is assisted by philanthropy over the years

The Vesperium stands as a testament to Joaquin Santos’ vision—a place where Filipino heritage is preserved, studied, and celebrated. As we continue to expand our collections, enhance our research facilities, and develop educational programs, we rely on the generosity of individuals who share our passion for history and innovation. Your support allows us to maintain this institution as a hub of knowledge, ensuring that future generations can access the rich cultural and intellectual legacy of the Philippines.

By contributing to the Vesperium, you become a vital part of our mission. Whether through donations, volunteering, or sponsorship, your support helps us curate new exhibitions, restore invaluable artifacts, and provide scholarships for aspiring scholars. Join us in safeguarding our nation’s past while shaping its future—every contribution makes a lasting impact.

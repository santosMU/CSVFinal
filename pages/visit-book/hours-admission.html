Welcome To, Vesperium



The Vesperium

open and welcoming visitors to the galleries everyday of the week varying from 10 or 11am to 6pm. Advanced booking is suggested but not required to ensure a slot in the museum without having to line up in queue and not have a sure chance inside. Walk-in visitors will be accomodated until slots last.

Holiday Hours

During holidays, the museum will only be open from 10 am- 3pm to ensure that the staff members are able to enjoy the holiday and spend their time with their families after the open hours of the museum.

Free Admissions

We give free admission to those who fit the following criteria:
- Senior Citizen
- Children ages 5 and below
- PWD Card holder
- Military Soldiers
- Institutional Members
- Family of Corporate Members
